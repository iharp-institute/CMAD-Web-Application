Convolution Matrix Anomaly Detection (CMAD)

Antarctic Anomalous Melting Detection Tool

This tool detects anomalous melting patterns across the entire Antarctic region using daily satellite imagery from NOAA NSIDC v4.0.

Instead of manually uploading images, simply enter two dates in YYYYMMDD format. The system will:

  • Automatically download official NOAA Antarctic sea ice images
  • Crop the full Antarctic region
  • Compare Day t with baseline Day t-n
  • Detect anomalous melting areas
  • Generate a CMAD anomaly visualization

Please ensure:

  • Both dates are in YYYYMMDD format
  • Baseline date (t-n) is earlier than target date (t)
  • Dates fall within available NOAA satellite records
